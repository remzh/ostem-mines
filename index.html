Icon Guide

restaurant Free Food

Free food will be provided at this event. In most cases, you will have to RSVP ahead of time in order to guarantee food.

confirmation_number RSVP / Application Required

This event requires you to RSVP and/or apply for in advance. Walk-ins will not be allowed.

award_star Big Event

A larger, typically more involved oSTEM event that occurs no more than twice per semester. If you only have time for a couple of things, try to prioritize these!

event_repeat Recurring Meeting

A low stakes, regularly scheduled oSTEM meeting. Recurring meetings generally occur once a week and are a great way to meet new people! RSVPs are not needed for recurring meetings (but may be required for food).

storefront Booth

We'll be tabling somewhere on campus, and we encourage you to volunteer some time with us if you can!
